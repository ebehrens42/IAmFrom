I Am From...
By: Ella Behrens


I am from my green tin cup and instant coffee
From my designated spot on the couch and my yellow pillow
I am from the 26th floor of my parents old, brick house
I am from the english ivy and the willow that never seemed to weep
I'm from pinball tournaments and screaming over card games
From Biggie G and Breezy Cheese
I'm from Turkey Trots and sand castles
and from shih tzus snuggled on the couch
I'm from "Hurry up" and "We're always late for school!"
I'm from lakefront runs and Washington Park races
I'm from downtown Chicago and rural Iowa
I'm from hotdogs on the beach and Applebees
From riding on my mom's handlebars
and basketball games with my dad
I am from the photos on my wall that remind me every day
of the joy and the love that I've felt in this life.
